# Quick Variation — pm-delivery

`depth = quick`. Deliver the essentials of the routed topic fast, without
sacrificing the non-negotiable gates.

## Behavior
- Resolve one topic, load one playbook, run the spine at essentials level.
- Produce the core deliverable only: the headline register/estimate/grid/plan,
  not every optional appendix.
- Still mandatory even in quick mode:
  - Evidence tags on every non-trivial claim.
  - FTE-months only for cost/budget/vendor — no prices.
  - For risk-assessment: still score Severity × Likelihood and name mitigations
    for High risks (you may compress Low risks to a single line).
  - For cost-estimation: still give optimistic/expected/pessimistic — a single
    point is never acceptable, even in quick mode.

## Example trigger
"Quick risk read on the payments migration — top risks and mitigations only."
→ Route risk-assessment, depth=quick: emit a trimmed register surfacing the
High/Medium rows with mitigations, mark out-of-scope categories N/A.
